- Regulation (EU) 2017/745 – Medical Device Regulation (MDR)
- Regulation (EU) 2016/679 – General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- EU Artificial Intelligence Act (High-Risk AI Systems)
- Selected international and harmonized standards, including:
  - ISO 13485

  - IEC 62366
  - ISO/IEC 27001
