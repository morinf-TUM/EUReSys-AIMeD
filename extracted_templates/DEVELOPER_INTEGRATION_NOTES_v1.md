# Developer Integration Notes

- The Global Disclaimer must be visible in the app footer and onboarding flow.
- The Per-Template Disclaimer must be injected verbatim into every generated document.
- No template may be exported without the disclaimer block.
- Version all documents and disclaimers independently.
- Maintain an audit log for user exports.
- Do not present any content as “certification-ready” or “guaranteed compliant.”
- Avoid any UX wording implying legal or regulatory authority.
