The AI Act Classification of High-risk also seems reasonable for the CardioDiagnosis AI device. The requirement for a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) under GDPR is also likely appropriate for the CardioDiagnosis AI device. As per Article 35 of the GDPR, a DPIA is required when processing is likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons. The processing of sensitive health data, as is the case with the CardioDiagnosis AI device, is considered a high risk under GDPR, necessitating a DPIA.
