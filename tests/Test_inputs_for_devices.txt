Smartwatch with electrodes
Monitor cardiac electrical activity to achieve monitoring on par with ECG, so as to anticipate adverse cardiac events.
 
The AI algorithms were trained on proprietary data collected through collaboration with a European RTO (Tecnalia) following all established ethical protocols.
The data is first embedded with the help of a Hilbert-Huang transform, then processed with a transformer
The data will be end-to-end encrypted between the watch and the designated physician's computer, who will be bound by GDPR.
